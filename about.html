The perimeters of modern organizations are disappearing. More and more businesses are built on cross-organizational collaboration, while people with different origins and identities are working on common projects, and are no longer confined in fixed office locations. As "cloud-first, mobile-first" becomes a core business strategy, the traditional approach of building network infrastructure based on perimeter not only is severely challenged in terms of ease of use, maintainability and security, but also limits the efficiency and possibilities of business development.

We believe the IT infrastructure of enterprises and organizations is evolving from "network-centric" to "business-centric" and "application-centric", and security is not a reason to restrict the business, but an ensurance to liberate and empower the business.

We are committed to delivering IT infrastructure solutions of the new generation, and power businesses and organizations of all kinds to transcend Mobile and Cloud.
