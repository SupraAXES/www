Data Loss Prevention

Data has become the core asset of enterprises. Whether it's customer information accumulated in daily operations or various operational data obtained during business operations, it is crucial for the continuous development and maintenance of a competitive advantage for enterprises. Simultaneously, data services have evolved into independent business models, providing data to customers and supporting user decisions forms the foundation of data service businesses. On the other hand, hackers, internal employees, and customers often have the ability to use automated tools to extract relevant business data, leaking it to competitors or engaging in secondary sales, causing significant harm to businesses.

SupraAXES Secure Workspace offers a comprehensive Data Protection solution that fundamentally prevents data extraction or leakage. Through SupraAXES Secure Workspace, pages, files, and data of data applications are projected and presented to users in image form, ensuring that the actual user experience of accessing relevant data applications remains unchanged, all while eliminating concerns about the original data provided by the application being extracted or leaked.

Resource Access & Protection

Due to business needs, enterprises and organizations often require certain legacy application systems to continue running and be accessible to users. However, due to changes in the original suppliers or even their closure, it has become difficult to obtain technical updates and security patches, turning these systems into management black holes and security blind spots.

SupraAXES Secure Workspace offers a simple, convenient, and comprehensive solution. Through SupraAXES Secure Workspace, users will be able to interact directly with a holographic projection of the business application through a web browser, without the need to install client software or perform specialized configurations. This provides users with the same experience as directly accessing the business application. Additionally, SupraAXES Secure Workspace presents the business application to users in a holographic projection, completely shielding the basic information and security vulnerabilities of the application, thereby fundamentally eliminating security threats.
